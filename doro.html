Doro Wot ❤️

Spicy Ethiopian Chicken Stew 🍗

Ingredients

Chicken pieces
Oil or butter
Onions
Berbere spice
Garlic & ginger
Boiled eggs

Steps

1 Cook chopped onions until dry
2 Add oil, garlic, ginger, and berbere
3 Add chicken pieces and cook well
4 Pour water and simmer slowly
5 Add boiled eggs and cook until thick
