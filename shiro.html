Shiro Wot ❤️

Delicious Ethiopian Chickpea Stew 🍲

Ingredients

Shiro (chickpea flour)
Oil or butter
Onions
Berbere spice
Garlic
Water

Steps

1 Cook chopped onions until soft
2 Add oil, garlic, and berbere
3 Mix shiro powder with water
4 Add to pot and stir continuously
5 Cook until thick and smooth
