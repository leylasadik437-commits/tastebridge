Spicy Ethiopian Injera Stir 🍞🔥

Ingredients

Injera (torn pieces)
Oil or butter
Onions
Berbere spice
Garlic
Tomato

Steps

1 Cook chopped onions until soft
2 Add oil, garlic, and berbere
3 Add chopped tomatoes and cook
4 Mix in torn injera pieces
5 Stir well until fully coated
